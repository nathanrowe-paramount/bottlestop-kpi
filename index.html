Executive Summary

This document establishes the performance framework for Bottle Stop Store Managers (SMs) effective FY27. The structure links bonus outcomes to six core business priorities: Commercial performance, Compliance discipline, Store Hygiene, People development, Customer Engagement, and Private Label growth.

Hard Reset Periods H1 (Jan-Jun) and H2 (Jul-Dec) create clear performance windows
Monthly Payments SMs receive bonuses monthly based on achieved tier
6-Month True-Up Underpayments corrected at period-end if average tier exceeds monthly payouts
Forecast-Driven All metrics benchmarked against Area Manager (AM) forecasts

Forecast Setting Process

By the 25th of each month, AMs submit next-month forecasts for:

  1. Revenue and GP $ (based on trading calendar, local events, promotional activity)
  2. Wages % target (staffing roster plan)
  3. Loyalty scan rate and sign-up targets
  4. Private label GP and sales targets

Forecast approval: Retail GM reviews all forecasts by month-end. Once set, forecasts only change in exceptional circumstances.

The Catch-Up Mechanism (True-Up)

Purpose: Reward SMs who improve performance over the half-year without penalising early underperformance.

How it works:
  1. Each month, SM receives provisional bonus payment based on achieved tier
  2. At end of H1 (June 30) and H2 (Dec 31), calculate average tier across 6 months
  3. If average tier exceeds sum of monthly payments, pay delta as lump sum
  4. If average is lower, no clawback — SM keeps provisional payments
