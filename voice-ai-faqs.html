How can I see my discounts on your website?

Your discounts appear in the Case Disc field next to each product. When you click into a product, the case price already includes the discount, and the unit price does too if the item is available to purchase individually. What you see is the final price you will be charged on your invoice.

Why have I been charged a Minimum Delivery Top-Up Charge?

A Minimum Delivery Top-Up Charge applies when the calculated freight on your order is less than the minimum delivery charge for your area. Your calculated freight is based on what you have ordered, such as the number of cases or kegs in the order. For metro deliveries, the minimum charge is usually $15, but some regional or rural areas may have a higher minimum charge. For example, if the calculated freight on your order is $10 and the minimum delivery charge for your area is $15, a Minimum Delivery Top-Up Charge of $5 is added to bridge the gap. Platinum and Gold loyalty customers are not charged a Minimum Delivery Top-Up Charge. If your order meets the required threshold, or your account is on Platinum or Gold loyalty status, this charge does not apply.

Why have I been charged again for a fix up order?

If an item on your original order was damaged or missing, a credit is issued against the original invoice for that item. If replacement stock is then sent out, it is processed on a new fix up order and charged on a separate invoice. That is why you may see another charge, even though the original invoice has already been credited for the damaged or missing item.
